Privacy Policy

LabNexusHealth — The Operating System for Modern Diagnostics

1. Introduction

LabNexusHealth provides software infrastructure for diagnostic laboratories and healthcare operations. Our platform includes the Lab Operations Platform, the Patient Mobile Application, the Doctor Referral Application, the Doctor CRM, Referral Management, Commission & Payout Management, Reports & Notifications, and Analytics & Business Insights.

LabNexusHealth is a technology company. We are not a diagnostic laboratory, healthcare provider, hospital, or medical practitioner. We do not provide medical advice, diagnostic services, or healthcare. Diagnostic services and patient care are delivered by the laboratories and healthcare providers who use our software.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use our platform and applications, how we use it, who we share it with, and the choices and rights available to you. By using LabNexusHealth, you agree to the practices described here.

Much of the health and patient information processed through our platform belongs to and is controlled by the laboratory or healthcare provider that uses our software. For questions about how a specific laboratory handles your information, please contact that laboratory directly. This Policy covers how LabNexusHealth, as the platform provider, handles information.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information, depending on how you use the platform and your role:

Account and contact information. Name, email address, mobile number, business or laboratory name, designation, and login credentials of users who register on the platform.

Patient information. When a laboratory uses our platform to manage its operations, it may enter or generate patient information such as name, age, gender, contact details, test bookings, sample details, diagnostic reports, and related records.

Doctor and referral information. Doctor names, qualifications, contact details, referral activity, and commission or payout details where referral and commission features are used.

Payment and billing information. Subscription and transaction records. Online payments are processed by third-party payment gateways; we do not store complete card or banking credentials.

Usage and device information. Device type, browser, operating system, app version, IP address, log-in activity, and how you interact with the platform.

Communications. Records of support requests and messaging activity (such as report and notification delivery via SMS, email, or WhatsApp).

We collect only the information needed to operate the platform and the services you and your laboratory use.

3. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the LabNexusHealth platform and applications.
  • Enable core functions such as test bookings, report generation and delivery, referral management, and commission and payout tracking.
  • Send transactional notifications, including booking confirmations, report-ready alerts, payment receipts, and service messages.
  • Authenticate users, secure accounts, and prevent fraud or misuse.
  • Provide customer support and respond to enquiries.
  • Manage subscriptions, billing, and payments.
  • Improve and develop platform features, including through analytics.
  • Comply with applicable laws and respond to lawful requests.

We do not sell personal information. We do not use patient health information for advertising. Promotional messages, where sent, are only sent with appropriate consent and you can opt out at any time.

4. Patient & Doctor Data

Patient and doctor information entered into the platform belongs to the laboratory or healthcare provider using LabNexusHealth. That organization is responsible for collecting this information lawfully, providing required notices, and obtaining any necessary consent from patients and doctors.

LabNexusHealth processes this information only to provide the platform and its features to the laboratory. We do not access, use, or disclose patient or doctor data for our own purposes, except as needed to operate, support, and secure the platform, or as required by law.

Patients and doctors who want to access, correct, or delete their information should contact the laboratory or healthcare provider they interact with, as that organization controls the record. We will support that organization in responding to such requests.

Diagnostic reports, medical interpretations, and patient communications are created and managed by the laboratory or healthcare provider. LabNexusHealth provides only the software used to store, generate, and deliver them.

5. Cookies & Analytics

Our website and web applications use cookies and similar technologies to keep you signed in, remember your preferences, keep the platform secure, and understand how the platform is used so we can improve it.

We use these categories of cookies:

  • Essential cookies, required for the platform to function and stay secure.
  • Functional cookies, which remember your settings and preferences.
  • Analytics cookies, which help us measure usage and improve performance.

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Disabling some cookies may affect how the platform works. We may use analytics tools to understand aggregate usage trends; these do not identify you personally.

6. Data Sharing

We share information only as needed to run the platform and as described below:

With your laboratory or organization. Information you enter is made available to the laboratory or healthcare provider whose account you use, and to its authorized users.

With service providers. We use trusted third parties for hosting, cloud storage, messaging (SMS, email, WhatsApp), payment processing, and analytics. They are permitted to use the information only to provide services to us and are required to protect it.

For legal reasons. We may disclose information where required by law, court order, or a valid request from a government authority, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of users, the public, or LabNexusHealth.

In a business transfer. If LabNexusHealth is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to this Policy.

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information. We may use aggregated or anonymized data that does not identify any individual to understand usage and improve our services.

7. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest.
  • Role-based access controls and unique user credentials.
  • OTP or multi-factor authentication where applicable.
  • Monitoring, logging, and regular security updates.
  • Secure, access-controlled hosting infrastructure.
  • Regular backups and recovery procedures.

No online system can be guaranteed completely secure. While we take strong measures to protect your information, you are responsible for keeping your login credentials confidential and for notifying us promptly of any suspected unauthorized access to your account.

8. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as it is needed to provide the platform, support your account, meet legal and accounting obligations, and resolve disputes.

Patient and operational data entered by a laboratory is retained while that laboratory's account is active. When an account is closed, we delete or return such data in line with the laboratory's instructions and applicable law, except where we are legally required to keep it for longer. Routine backups containing data are retained for our standard backup cycle and then deleted or overwritten.

Account, billing, and security records that we hold as the platform provider are kept for as long as required for our legitimate business and legal purposes, after which they are deleted or anonymized.

9. User Rights

Subject to applicable Indian law, you may:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request deletion of your information, where it is no longer required and we are not legally obliged to retain it.
  • Withdraw consent for processing that is based on consent, without affecting processing already carried out.
  • Raise a grievance or complaint about how your information is handled.

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details in Section 12. We may need to verify your identity before acting on a request, and we will respond within the time required by law, and in any case within 30 days. If your request concerns information controlled by a laboratory or healthcare provider, we will direct you to, or assist, that organization.
